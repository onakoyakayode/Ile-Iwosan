WHAT ARE THE STEPS?

  • STEPS FOR IN-PERSON SERVICES
  • STEPS FOR REMOTE MED-ON-DEMAND SERVICES

    • STEP ONE: CONTACT US

    Click on the “Book an In-person Appointment/ Check-up Now” and fill in a brief form with your preferred hospital, date and time and the type of specialist you would like to consult with or contact us via WhatsApp.

    STEP TWO: INITIAL CONTACT

    An ILE-IWOSAN Case Manager will get back to you to collect any available medical records related to the condition. The specialists at our partner hospitals use these to form a second opinion, come up with accurate treatment options and costs for treatment.

    STEP THREE: PLANNING

    An ILE-IWOSAN client coordinator then takes the client through the medical treatment options provide by the specialist, fees and costs for accommodation to help the client to make an informed decision.

    STEP FOUR: TREATMENT

    Once a client has confirmed they want to proceed with the treatment, a client coordinator makes all the arrangements: books the treatment with our partner hospital, books suitable accommodation close to the hospital.

    We also go that extra step to ensure that our client has a trouble-free trip by staying in communication throughout the treatment and helping to resolve any thing that may come up.
